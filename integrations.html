How each integration works

G

Google Calendar

Connect any Google Workspace or personal Google account. ReachAI reads free/busy times for each staff member you select, and creates events on the right calendar when bookings come in.

Setup time: 30 seconds
Scopes: calendar.read, calendar.events
Sync latency: <500ms via push channels
Best for: Teams already on Google Workspace
O

Microsoft Outlook

Works with Microsoft 365 personal and business accounts. Reads availability across multiple staff calendars and writes new appointments with full attendee details.

Setup time: 45 seconds
Auth: Microsoft Graph OAuth
Sync latency: Webhook + delta queries
Best for: M365 shops & enterprise
c

Cal.com

For teams running the open-source scheduler. Paste a single API key — ReachAI reads your event types, durations, and team availability and books real Cal.com appointments.

Setup time: 60 seconds
Auth: API key from settings
Supports: Self-hosted & cloud
Best for: Devs & technical teams
cL

Calendly

Paste your Calendly personal access token. ReachAI reads your event types and routing logic, then creates real Calendly bookings with all your existing rules respected.

Setup time: 60 seconds
Auth: Personal access token
Respects: Buffers, limits, round-robin
Best for: Service businesses & consultants
