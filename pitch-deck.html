CarePresence

Intelligent scheduling & patient access for behavioral health.

Turn real calendar constraints into self-service booking across chat and voice—without losing operational visibility.

Problem

Scheduling is the bottleneck

  • Phones and portals don’t reflect true availability.
  • Staff context-switch between EHR, inbox, and callbacks.
  • Patients abandon when the next step isn’t obvious.
Solution

Availability-grounded access

CarePresence ingests schedule truth from your workflow (CSV today; APIs tomorrow), exposes it through AI-guided chat and voice, and notifies operations when a booking lands.
