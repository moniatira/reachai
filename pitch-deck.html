Customer pain points

What clinics and patients feel every week

Operations & staff

  • Phones ring while the schedule in the EHR already moved.
  • Front desk toggles between CSV exports, spreadsheets, and “who called back?”
  • No-show risk rises when booking isn’t tied to true availability.

Patients & access

  • Portal slots don’t match what schedulers know is real.
  • After-hours = voicemail, not self-service against live rules.
  • Anxiety and ADHD populations need low-friction paths—not ten clicks.
Current market gaps

Where today’s tools stop short

  • Schedule truth is fragmented. EHR calendars, provider preferences, and templates rarely surface cleanly to a patient-facing channel.
  • Generic chatbots aren’t constraint-aware. They suggest times without honoring busy blocks, modality rules, or intake logic.
  • Voice & messaging aren’t unified. IVR, SMS, and web booking live in different stacks—reporting and handoffs break.
  • Compliance is bolted on late. Pilot-friendly demos ignore BAA, retention, and audit until it’s expensive to retrofit.

How CarePresence is different

Differentiation

Availability-first: Imports schedule truth from exportable workflows today (CSV → structured busy/free); API paths later—so AI never “invents” a slot.

Same journey, every channel: One booking graph for chat and voice—reduces abandonment and training load.

Operational visibility: Confirmations surface for front desk without digging through threads.

Enterprise-ready path: Designed for PHI boundaries, identity, and audit as part of deployment—not as an afterthought.
