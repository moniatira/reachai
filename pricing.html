FAQ

What counts as a "booking"?

A booking is any confirmed appointment created on your calendar. Reschedules and cancellations don't count against your quota. Conversations that don't result in a booking are unlimited.

Why is the chat widget custom?

Your customers see your brand, not ours. The widget pulls your colors and logo from your website automatically, and you can customize the greeting, the AI personality, even the chat bubble icon. It looks like part of your site — not a generic third-party chatbot.

Do I get a phone number?

Yes. Every plan includes a US phone number at no extra cost. You can also forward your existing business line to it.

How does the 14-day trial work?

No credit card required. Use the full Pro plan for 14 days. After day 14 you choose a paid plan or your account pauses — no surprise charges.

What if I go over my booking limit?

We never block bookings. We email you to upgrade and bill $1 per booking over your limit until you do.

Can I bring my own AI / voice provider?

On the Scale plan you can plug in your own OpenAI, Anthropic, or ElevenLabs keys. Useful for compliance-sensitive industries.
