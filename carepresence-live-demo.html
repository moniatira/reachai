Turn scheduling data into live patient access

CarePresence connects your calendar reality to AI chat and voice booking—so patients self-serve against real availability, and your front desk stays in the loop. This pilot preview uses illustrative data for stakeholder demos.

EHR-aware schedule import

Import CSV exports from common EHR workflows. Busy times block the calendar; open slots surface automatically for booking.

Guided AI booking

Natural-language flow captures reason for visit, modality, and time—aligned to your rules before confirmation.

Voice & omnichannel

Same state machine over voice or text so patients can complete intake-style booking hands-free when appropriate.

Front-desk visibility

Confirmations surface operationally so staff see new bookings without digging through the EHR.

Built for production conversations. Deployments can integrate enterprise auth, PHI boundaries, audit logging, and your compliance review—this browser preview does not store PHI and uses sample content only.
Pilot disclaimer: Not a medical device. Not for diagnosis or treatment decisions. Illustrative data only; configure retention, BAA, and security with your team before production use.
